1. Reconnaissance was performed to gather information about the target domain using OSINT tools.

2. Tools used include Maltego, Shodan, and WHOIS.

3. WHOIS provided domain registration details like registrar and name servers.

4. Wappalyzer identified technologies such as HTTP/2 and Cloudflare CDN.

5. crt.sh revealed certificate details and wildcard subdomains (*.example.com).

6. Wildcard subdomains indicate the presence of multiple hidden or unlisted subdomains.

7. Shodan identified exposed services and devices across multiple countries.

8. Common open ports found were 80 (HTTP) and 443 (HTTPS).

9. Some systems used self-signed certificates and included honeypots.

10. Maltego was used for asset mapping to visualize domain, DNS, and IP relationships.
